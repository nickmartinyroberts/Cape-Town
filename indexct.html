Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town is undoubtedly one of the worlds greatest cities. Nestled in between the Indian Ocean and the majestic Table Mountain, Cape Town's beauty is rivalled only by its cosmopolitan culture. With the famed winelands producing internationally awarded vintages coupled with the cities sporting history, there is literally something for everyone.

Table Mountain

Table Mountain is the most iconic mountain in Southern Africa. Proudly overlooking the bay of Cape Town, Table Mountain is also one of the most accessible. Visitors can choose to take the well marked hiking path to the east of the mountain or use the cable car to the south.

The V and A Waterfront

It is perhaps at the V and A waterfront that Cape Town really proves what a modern and advanced country South Africa has become. Whether it is Louis Vuitton or Sports Direct, the shopping district has it all. Here are some of the other options:

  • V and A Cinema

Accomodation for all budgets

When travelling to Cape Town you can be safe in the knowledge that there will be a plethora of accommodation for every budget. Whether it is the funky backpackers on Long Street to the infamous celebrity hang-out, the Twelve Apostles, you will be well catered for.

  • the Twelve Apostles, 5 star
  • the One and Only, 5 star
